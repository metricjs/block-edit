About BlockEdit

Have you ever been in the middle of writing something - a paper, a novel, or something else - and realised you've structured it wrong? You read through what you've written and identify all the parts that need to be rearranged, and then you spend several hours cutting and pasting sections until it's all in the right order.

Then, a few hours later you realise it's still not correct and you have to repeat the process all over again...
